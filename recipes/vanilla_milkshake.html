Vanilla Milkshake

Description

A vanilla milkshake is a classic dessert drink made with vanilla ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract. It's a simple and delicious treat that can be enjoyed on its own or paired with a variety of toppings like whipped cream, chocolate syrup, or sprinkles.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients to a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Pour into a glass and serve
