Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Fried rice is a traditional Chinese preparation of cooked rice, vegetables, protein, soy sauce, and aromatics. The ingredients are stir-fried in a large pan or wok for even flavor distribution. An ideal use for leftovers, fried rice is quick, customizable, and incredibly simple to put together with whatever is in your fridge.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 cup of protein (chicken, pork, shrimp, or tofu)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add protein and cook until browned.
  3. Push protein to the side and add mixed vegetables to the pan.
  4. Push vegetables to the side and scramble eggs in the pan.
  5. Add cooked rice to the pan and stir to combine.
  6. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper to the pan.
  7. Stir everything together and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot.
