Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

Mac and cheese is a classic American comfort food made with cooked pasta and a cheesy sauce. The sauce is typically made with a combination of butter, flour, milk, and cheese, and can be customized with different types of cheese or additional ingredients like bacon or vegetables. Baked mac and cheese is a popular variation that includes a crispy breadcrumb topping.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz of pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup of breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Whisk in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Gradually whisk in milk until smooth.
  5. Bring to a simmer and cook until thickened.
  6. Stir in cheese, salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder until melted.
  7. Combine pasta and cheese sauce in a baking dish.
  8. Sprinkle breadcrumbs on top.
  9. Bake at 350Â°F for 20-25 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
  10. Let cool slightly before serving.
